Smithsonian National Museum of American History

The Smithsonian National Museum of American Historytells the story of the US since settlers arrived from Europe, with exhibitions covering the social, political, scientific, cultural and military history of the country. The exhibitions give an interesting look at the building of a nation and how this changed the world at large during its comparatively short history.
